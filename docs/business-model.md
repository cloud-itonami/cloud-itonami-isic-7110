# Business Model: Community Architectural and Engineering Practice

## Classification
- Repository: `cloud-itonami-7110`
- ISIC Rev.5: `7110` — architectural and engineering practice — design, simulation, verification and certification
- Social impact: built-environment safety professional-access

## Customer
- small practices, cooperatives and public works programs needing auditable design records

## Offer
- scope and design intake, CAE/CFD verification, drawing and spec records, certification evidence, audit

## Revenue
- setup fee per practice, monthly operations subscription, certification and integration services

## Trust Controls
- design outside code/scope is blocked; verification evidence is required; certification is auditable
- a robot action the governor refuses is never dispatched to hardware
- every dispatch, hold, approval and disclosure path is auditable
- sensitive operating and personal data stays outside Git
